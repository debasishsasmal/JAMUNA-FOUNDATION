About the Campaign

Our Tree Plantation Drive is a community initiative to combat pollution and promote a healthier environment. We aim to plant over 1,000 saplings across Bhubaneswar with the help of dedicated volunteers like you.

Joining is easy! Simply register for our next event, show up on the day, and we'll provide you with all the necessary tools and guidance. Every hand helps!
